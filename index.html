I am currently studying Business Technology Management at Toronto Metropolitan University. The Business Technology Management (BTM) program teaches students analytical and problem-solving skills to create business solutions using Information and Communication Technologies.

Some course's I'm currently taking this fall:

  1. RTA 928 Introduction to Game Design

    This course covers the basics of game design and delves into the design of, the game world, character, interactivity, navigation, and gameplay mechanics.

  2. ITM 780 Web Design and Management

    This course is an intro to web design and provides students with the skills to develop design, and manage websites.

  3. SOC 902 Hollywood and Society

    This course delves into the ideas and discussion topics related to Hollywood and society, and how culture impacts films and their interpretations.

  4. ITM 750 IS Project Management

    This course provides a high-level understanding of the tools and techniques utilized by project managers when planning and executing projects.

  5. ITM 707 Strategy, Management, and Acquisition

    This course focuses on the utilization of Information Systems (IS), when analyzing, planning, and executing organizational targets.
